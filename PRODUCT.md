# PRODUCT.md — Wellness Weekend

## Register
**Brand Surface** — This is a marketing/event site where design IS the product. Every pixel sells the experience.

## Audience
Spiritually-curious millennials and Gen-X seekers (28–50), primarily women, who invest in retreats, plant medicine, and healing arts. They browse on mobile between yoga classes or late at night researching their next transformational experience. They've been to Burning Man or Wanderlust but want something more intimate. They judge quality by feel — if the site doesn't *feel* premium and intentional, they bounce.

## Brand Voice (3 words)
**Sacred. Wild. Intentional.**

## Visual References
- Erewhon website (earthy luxury meets clean editorial)
- Wanderlust Festival (spiritual-meets-modern branding)
- Moon Juice (psychedelic-earthy premium wellness)
- Apple product pages (pacing, white space, scroll-driven reveals)

## Anti-References
- Eventbrite templates (generic, transactional)
- Burning Man site (chaotic, over-decorated)
- GoFundMe pages (desperate, cluttered)
- Generic yoga studio sites (stock photos, comic sans energy)

## Core Conversion Goals
1. Ticket purchases via Square checkout
2. Email capture as a fallback for visitors who are not ready to buy
3. Travel, lodging, inclusion, and policy clarity that removes purchase risk
4. Vendor, volunteer, sponsor, and affiliate paths as secondary routes
